About

Everything you need to know

On this site, you can find a simple chess interface and some resources on the game of chess. The interface does not validate legal moves, which renders the experience closer to playing a physical over the board game.

Features

  • Drag pieces to move
  • Drag the bottom corner of the board to adjust the size
  • Press 'f' to flip the board (or use the settings menu)
  • Scores can be updated in the settings menu and persist across sessions
  • Pawns can be promoted
  • Pieces can be manually removed (for en passant)
  • Board can be reset
  • Pieces snap into place
  • Sound plays when pieces are moved
